To solve a Super Metroid randomized ROM click on Solve!.

To randomize a ROM with the VARIA randomizer click on Randomize!.

Randomizers

There are three items randomizers for Super Metroid:

  • The tournament randomizer (online)
  • Dessyreqt randomizer
  • Very Adaptive Randomizer of Items and Areas (VARIA) randomizer (online)
