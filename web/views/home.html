The VARIA website is divided in three parts:

  • manage your preset
  • generate randomized Super Metroid ROMs
  • solve randomized Super Metroid ROMs (from VARIA/Total/Dessy randomizers)

At the center of the VARIA experience is the preset.
A preset contains the list of all the techniques that you know in Super Metroid with your perceived difficulty.

To create and manage your preset click on Presets.

Use your preset to generate randomized Super Metroid ROMs that will only required techniques that you know.
The items locations are randomized, you can also randomize accesses between areas.
To randomize a ROM with the VARIA Randomizer click on Randomizer.

Using your preset you can check if you can finish a Super Metroid randomized ROM and get an estimation of the difficulty.
The Solver also provides a spoiler path to follow will the techniques/items used at each locations.
To solve a Super Metroid randomized ROM click on Solver.
