Welcome to Very Adaptive Randomizer of Items and Areas for Super Metroid

Here, you will be able to generate as many VARIAtions of Super Metroid as you want, by Randomizing Item locations, and even the connections between the main Areas!
If you're not an uberskilled speedrunner, have no fear, neither are we. It is Very Adaptive to all skill levels, and you are guaranteed to be able to finish a randomized ROM, granted that you create your skill preset, or use a standard one in your range.
To find your way in these sometimes not so easy randomized ROMs (aka 'seeds') you will be able to use a Solver, to estimate the seed's difficulty or have a full spoiler log. Even better, you can use the interactive Item Tracker to help you as you play along. For area-randomized seeds, the Tracker can also be used in a more simple way just to help you keep track of the area connections without any additional help.

Presets
  • At the center of the VARIA experience is the preset
  • All the techniques which can be required in a randomized Super Metroid ROM are listed
  • For each technique you can tell if you know it and your perceived difficulty
  • Also take into account the hard rooms (Gauntlet, Xray) and the bosses
  • Standards presets of increased difficulty are included, as a base to create your one
  • A skill level bar allow you to compare your preset to the standards and community presets
  • Tutorial available for most of the techniques
  • The rooms where the technique can be used are listed
  • Put your gamepad mapping in your preset

Randomizer
  • Use your preset to generate randomized Super Metroid ROMs that will only required techniques that you know
  • Full item randomization (like Dessy) or majors/minors randomization (like Total)
  • Area randomization: randomize the access between the main areas of the game
  • Different progression speed, from slowest, stuffing your way with as many non-progression items as possible, to fastest, where you will be able to visit Zebes pretty shortly
  • Choose the distribution of minors
  • Choose the quantity of minors and energy
  • Various optional patches are available to enhance the gameplay
  • Super Fun Times: Have fun randomly removing major item

Solver
  • Using your preset you can check if you can finish a Super Metroid randomized ROM (Dessy/Total/VARIA randomizers are supported) and get an estimation of the difficulty.
  • Display an optional spoiler giving you a realistic path to follow to finish the seed.
  • All the techniques and items used to reach a location are displayed
  • You can click on an item to highlight all occurences of it in the spoiler for easy spotting
  • The solver can collect all items or only the minimal required
  • Give a maximum difficulty for visiting locations

Area and Item Trackers
  • Area tracker: add lines between the area portals when playing an Area randomized seed
  • Interactive Item tracker: the available locations are displayed on the map, click on them to collect the item and display the next available locations
  • The current state of the trackers is automatically saved, allowing you to finish your seed in multiple seatings
