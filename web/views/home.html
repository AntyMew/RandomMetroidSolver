Welcome to the home of the VARIA (Very Adaptive Randomizer of Items and Areas) Randomizer and Solver for Super Metroid.

Here you'll be able to manage your preset of the techniques you know, generate some randomized Super Metroid ROMs and also solve them.

You can create your preset which contains the techniques you know and your preferences. The preset is used to randomize and to solve ROMs.
To create and manage your preset click on Presets.

To randomize a ROM with the VARIA Randomizer click on Randomizer.

To solve a Super Metroid randomized ROM click on Solver.
